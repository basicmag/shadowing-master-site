FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Shadowing Master?

Shadowing Master is a Chrome extension that helps you learn languages from Amazon Prime Video using dual subtitles, shadowing tools, and AI coaching.

How much does it cost?

The extension costs $2.99 per month and includes a 14-day free trial. You can cancel any time.

Which devices and services are supported?

Shadowing Master runs on the desktop version of Google Chrome and is optimized for Amazon Prime Video. It also supports imported SRT subtitle files.

Who is it for?

It is designed for language learners who want to study English or other languages through real movies and TV shows while keeping their native language visible.

Is Shadowing Master affiliated with Amazon?

No. Shadowing Master is an independent tool and is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by Amazon.
